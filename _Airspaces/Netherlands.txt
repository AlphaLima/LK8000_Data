*
*################################################################

* land areas or territorial waters of the Netherlands,    	#
* within which the flight of aircraft is prohibited.      	#
*							  	#
* This term is only used when the flight of civil aircraft	#
* within the designated airspace is not permitted at any  	#
* time under any circumstances.					#

*####### RESTRICTED AIRSPACE (R) ##########################
*
*##########################################################
* Definition: an airspace of defined dimensions, above the#
* land areas or territorial waters of the Netherlands, 	  #
* within which the flight of aircraft is restricted in    #
* accordance with certain specified conditions.		  #
*							  #
* This term is used whenever the flight of civil aircraft #
* within the designated airspace is not absolutely 	  #
* prohibited but may be made only if specified conditions #
* are complied with. Thus, prohibition of flight except at#
* certain specified times leads to the designation of the #
* airspace as a 'restricted area' as would prohibition    #
* except in certain meteorological conditions. Similarly, #
* prohibition of flight unless special permission has been#
* obtained, leads to the designation of a restricted area.#
*##########################################################
